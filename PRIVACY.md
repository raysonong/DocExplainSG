DocExplainSG helps you understand official Singapore documents (e.g. CPF, HDB,
IRAS, MOM, insurance, town council, utility letters) by producing a
plain-language explanation in your chosen language. This policy explains how we
handle your information, in line with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act
(PDPA).

## 1. What we collect

- **The document you submit** — the photo(s) or PDF you upload or capture.
- **Your chosen output language** (English / Mandarin / Malay / Tamil).

We do **not** ask for your name, account, email, or any sign-in. We do not use
cookies or trackers.

Your document may itself contain personal data (names, addresses, amounts,
reference numbers). See §6 on how we handle identity numbers.

## 2. Why we use it (purpose)

Solely to generate the plain-language explanation you requested, and to answer
follow-up questions you ask about that document. We do not use it for any other
purpose, and we do not sell or share it for marketing.

## 3. How it is processed, and who it is sent to

To generate the explanation, your document is sent to our processing partner,
**Anthropic, PBC (United States)**, which runs the Claude AI model. Specifically:

- Anthropic processes the document **only to return the explanation**.
- Under Anthropic's commercial terms, **your inputs and outputs are not used to
  train AI models**.
- `[CONFIRM: Data Processing Addendum signed and zero-data-retention (ZDR)
  requested — see PDPA_CHECKLIST.md]`

This involves a **transfer of data outside Singapore (to the US)**. We rely on
our contract with Anthropic to ensure a comparable standard of protection as
required by the PDPA's Transfer Limitation Obligation.

## 4. Storage and retention

- **On our side:** nothing is stored. Your document is processed in memory and
  discarded as soon as the explanation is returned. There is no account, no
  history, and we do not log the contents of your document — only non-sensitive
  operational metadata (timestamps, latency, error codes).
- **On Anthropic's side:** they may retain inputs/outputs for a limited period
  for safety and abuse monitoring, unless a zero-data-retention arrangement is
  in place. `[CONFIRM retention terms once the DPA/ZDR is finalised.]`

## 5. Security

We use reasonable security arrangements, including: processing in memory with no
persistence, no logging of document contents, and transmission over encrypted
connections (HTTPS/TLS). `[CONFIRM: HTTPS, API authentication, and rate limiting
are enabled in the production deployment.]`

## 6. Identity numbers (NRIC / FIN)

Consistent with PDPC's guidelines on NRIC and other national identification
numbers, the app is designed **not to surface your NRIC or FIN**. The model is
instructed to omit them, and a server-side safeguard removes any NRIC/FIN it
detects from the explanation and from displayed reference numbers. Because some
official reference numbers (e.g. an individual income-tax reference, or a
work-pass/FIN number) **are** identity numbers, those are removed too.

## 7. Consent

We ask for your consent before you submit any document, via an in-app notice you
must agree to. You may withdraw consent at any time by not submitting documents
and by discontinuing use of the app.

## 8. Your rights

As we do not store your documents or extracted data, there is generally no
retained personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. For questions about
your rights under the PDPA, contact our Data Protection Officer (§10).

## 9. This is an aid, not advice

Explanations are generated by an AI model and may contain errors. They are an
aid only and are **not** official or legal advice. Always check the original
document and contact the issuing agency if you are unsure.
